## People creating new layouts

* You may want to set `saveBackConfig` in `main.yml` to `true` while writing your layout.
* You should include all of the files that are relevant to your layout.
    * At least:
        * `actionEvents.yml`
        * `audioEvents.yml`
    * Should not include:
        * `main.yml`
